Jumps

Jumps are optional. They will not hurt your score. They will only help you earn more points.Acceptable Jumps are: Toe Touch, Pike, Left/Right Hurdler, Left/Right Hurkie. If you have learned different preps please try to learn these, if you can not any prep is acceptable for jumps.

Tumbling

You do not have to perform any type of tumbling. If you choose to do so, you will show your skill at your own risk. We will not provide any type of mat. Please be safe.

Dance
